The Young Bloods

They are living in an impossibility.

It's hard to survive in a land of magic and war. Two teens both balance on a knife's point, where any wrong move can spell death.

Saylea's a street fighter turned wanted criminal turned escaped convict—and now wanted criminal again. She lives knowing there’s a price on her head and a target on her back.

Shane's a street rat who didn’t mean to become the people’s savior—or a sharpshooting, assassinating double agent either. He lives knowing there’s blood on his hands and high stakes for redemption.

When their paths collide, they learn they are more similar than they think. Before the devastating night that ripped them from the only lives they knew, before the frightening morning that left them worlds away from home, they both were ordinary high schoolers in modern day New York City. And now they are somehow here, on a fantastical continent that fills each day with fear.

A man who calls himself the Keeper tells them he has the answers to how they got here—and how to get back. They just have to do him a favor: retrieve a mysterious object he needs.

It’s a favor that will send them on a harrowing journey through other worlds—for other worlds exist, and so many of them. A favor that will entangle them in a dangerous rivalry between the Keeper and his brother. A favor that will force them to tap into powers they never even knew they had.

Saylea and Shane just want to go home. But they may be responsible for the fate of the world in more ways than they ever thought possible.

Dark Pointe

Get ready for the cutthroat world of ballet. . . .

Seventeen-year-old Emmanuelle Sinclair is all set to be the Next Big Thing in ballet. She’s a triple threat with her talent (she’s the daughter of a world class dancer and a world class choreographer), her training (she’s the student of the most selective ballet school there is), and her track record of taking down whoever stands in her way.

But it wasn’t always like this. A year ago, fellow student and ex-best-friend Samantha Gray had been on the brink of outshining Emmanuelle.

Samantha’s not here anymore. Ever since the accident, she’s been off the grid. No one knows exactly what happened. Was she in the car that crashed or hit by it? What condition is she in?

There are rumors of course. That Samantha’s dead. That Samantha’s fine and plotting a grand return. That Samantha’s face had been shattered and reconstructed into something unrecognizable. That queen bee, control freak Emmanuelle had something to do with it.

Emmanuelle can’t say if that last rumor’s true: the night of the accident comes up as a blank slate in her memory. And that scares her. So she pretends it never happened.

Until a beheaded, bloodied ballerina statuette turns up in her locker. Emmanuelle recognizes it right away. Only a few people in the world have that one-of-a-kind statuette: the girls in her circle. And only one is unaccounted for.

More unsettling things happen. Things that shake Emmanuelle up. That undermine her power. Things like the arrival of a new student at school whose promise reminds Emmanuelle of Samantha—and of the rumors concerning Samantha’s new face.

Emmanuelle can’t stop pretending nothing has happened anymore. There are some secrets to unravel here—and Samantha Gray just might be at the center of them all.
